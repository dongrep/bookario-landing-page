Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Bookario This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Bookario mobile application (the "App") developed by Pushhub and owned by 4TechVentures. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Information We Collect When you use the Bookario App, we may collect the following types of personal information: User Registration Information: When you register an account with Bookario, we collect information such as your name, email address, and password to create and manage your account. Login Information: We collect information about your interactions with the App, including the date and time of your visits, the pages or screens viewed, and other actions taken within the App. Booking Details: When you make bookings through the App, we collect information such as the date, time, and details of the booking, including any preferences or special requests you may have. Transaction Information: If you make purchases or payments through the App, we collect information about the transaction, such as the payment method used, transaction amount, and other details necessary to process the payment. How We Use Your Information We use the personal information collected through the Bookario App for the following purposes: To provide and maintain the App's functionality and services. To process and fulfill bookings made through the App. To communicate with you regarding your account, bookings, transactions, and updates to our services. To personalize your experience and provide tailored content and recommendations. To improve the App, develop new features, and conduct data analysis to better understand user needs and preferences. To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or unauthorized access to the App. Information Sharing and Disclosure We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App and providing services to you, such as payment processors, hosting providers, and customer support services. These service providers are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide services to us. We may also share your information in response to legal requests or proceedings, to protect our rights or property, or to enforce our policies and agreements. Data Retention We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Your Rights You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored within the Bookario App. You may also choose to opt-out of certain data collection and processing activities, although this may impact your ability to use certain features of the App. Changes to This Privacy Policy We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the revised policy in the App or through other means of communication. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@bookario.com. This Privacy Policy was last updated on 8 May 2024.
